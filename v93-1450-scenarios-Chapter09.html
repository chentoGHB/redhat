Start, stop, restart, and reload system services

Intermediate Level
üîß Incident Response Scenario

Click to copy
Start a stopped service immediately
Requires root privileges, .service extension optional
#systemctl stop suspicious-daemon
Click to copy
Stop a running service immediately
Critical for incident response and malicious service termination

Click to copy
Stop and then start a service (full restart)

üö´ Service Masking & Security

Prevent services from starting for security hardening

Advanced Level
üõ°Ô∏è Security Hardening Scenario
Preventing conflicting mail services and legacy insecure services from ever starting, even accidentally, to maintain security posture
#systemctl mask telnet.service
Click to copy
Mask insecure service to prevent any startup
Creates symlink to /dev/null, completely prevents service activation
#systemctl mask sendmail.service
Click to copy
Prevent conflicting mail service from starting
Ensures only one mail service can be active (e.g., postfix vs sendmail)

Click to copy

Masking vs Disabling
Disabled: Won't start at boot but can be started manually ‚Ä¢ Masked: Cannot be started manually or automatically - complete prevention
