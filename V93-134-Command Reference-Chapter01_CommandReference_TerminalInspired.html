$grep -c "pattern" filename

Click to copy
$grep "^pattern" filename

^ is the line anchor for start of line
Click to copy
$grep "pattern$" filename
Match pattern at end of line
$ is the line anchor for end of line
Click to copy
$grep -n -A 3 -B 3 "pattern" filename
Show line numbers with 3 lines of context before and after

Click to copy
$grep -r "pattern" /path/to/directory

Click to copy
$grep -E "(pattern1|pattern2)" filename
Extended regex with alternation (OR)
-E enables extended regex syntax
Click to copy
$grep -e "pattern1" -e "pattern2" filename
